About me

I am a first-year PhD student supervised by Prof. Jiasi SHEN at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, HKUST.

My main area of research is software engineering and programming languages. And my research interests lie in program analysis, program transformations and program synthesis, and automated techniques enhancing developer productivity.

In my opinion, life is a constant process of compromise and transcendence, moreover a constant search for balance between what we want to do, what we can do, and what is worth doing.
