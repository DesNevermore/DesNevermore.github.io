About me

I am a PhD student advised by Prof. Jiasi SHEN in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology. Previously, I earned a master's degree from HKUST and a bachelor's degree from UESTC.

My main research area is programming languages and software engineering, with a particular interest in program synthesis and code generation. Specifically, I focus on exploring both symbolic and neural approaches and their synergy to advance the correctness, scalability, and generality of program synthesis. If you have any questions, feel free to contact me!
